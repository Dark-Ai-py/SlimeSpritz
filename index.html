About Us

Welcome to Slime Spritz, where we turn playtime into a sensory adventure! At Slime Spritz, we specialize in creating handcrafted slime products that inspire creativity and relaxation.


Our extensive collection includes pre-made slimes in a rainbow of colors, textures, and scents, as well as fully customizable options made to suit your individual preferences. From snowy slime slime to stretchy fluffy slime, there's something for everyone at Slime Spritz.


In addition to our online storefront, we offer exciting mobile slime events that bring the fun straight to your doorstep. Perfect for birthdays, our skilled slime artists lead interactive workshops that teach participants how to craft their very own personalized slime creations. With step-by-step guidance and endless possibilities, your group is sure to enjoy hours of entertainment and creative expression.


Slime Spritz was founded because we share a deep appreciation for experiences that encourage self-expression and foster connection between people. By combining artistry, innovation, and passion, we aim to provide unforgettable moments for families and communities.


Thank you for choosing Slime Spritz as your go-to source for all things slime. We can't wait to help you discover the magic of slime!
